Customer Spend Analysis Project

Understanding Spending Behavior for Business Insights

Project Overview

This project analyzes customer spending behavior across various categories, payment types, and age groups. Using SQL for analysis and Python for visualization, the project aims to deliver insights into spending trends and key performance indicators.

Project Goals:

  • Analyze spending by payment type
  • Identify top-performing categories
  • Segment customers by age group and city
  • Provide actionable business insights

Key Insights

  • Payment Methods: Credit cards dominate customer payments, with the highest average spending.
  • Top Categories: Bills and Groceries categories see the largest share of customer spending.
  • Customer Demographics: Salaried IT Employees in Bengaluru contribute significantly to spending.
  • Age Group Analysis: Customers aged 25-45 form the core of high-value transactions.
