Project Overview

The E-Commerce Sales Analysis project focuses on evaluating sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and regional contributions using Power BI. This interactive dashboard helps businesses identify growth opportunities and optimize decision-making.

Project Goals:

  • Understand sales trends and patterns.
  • Identify top-performing products and regions.
  • Segment customers based on their sales contributions.

Skills Implemented:

  • Advanced Power BI Dashboard Design
  • DAX Formulas for Calculations
  • Data Cleaning and Modeling
  • Interactive Visualizations
